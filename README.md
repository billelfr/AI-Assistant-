# AI-Assistant-

"Summary
We're building Nudgly (Nudge - Lee), an AI-powered personal assistant that helps people manage their day — tasks, reminders, calendar, email, routines, and bookings, all through natural conversation on a web dashboard and WhatsApp.

The core idea: the user talks, the agent understands, and it actually takes action on their behalf. Not a chatbot that answers questions, an assistant that does the work.


Where we are

We have a working foundation and a clear build plan. The first phases are done, the core agent, integrations, and inbox are in place. We started with a developer who delivered the early phases, but we've reached a point where the quality and pace aren't matching what the next stage of this product needs.

We're now looking for a more experienced engineer to take this forward properly.


What we need you to be strong at

This is the part that matters most. The next phases are about real agentic execution, the agent breaking a goal into steps, deciding what it can do on its own, doing it, and checking in with the user at the right moments. We need someone who has actually built this kind of thing before, not someone learning it on our project.

Specifically, you should be comfortable with:

Building reliable AI agents that take real actions (not just generate text)

Multi-step / orchestrated agent workflows with approval gates for sensitive actions

Tool-calling architectures where behavior is enforced in code, not left to the model
Integrating LLMs cleanly into a production app


What the assistant needs to do:

Manage tasks, reminders, calendar events, and email 
through conversation

Run recurring routines and multi-step automations ("every Friday, summarize my week and send it to me")

Take on a project, break it into tasks, and work on the parts it can handle

Do web research, draft documents, and send communications on the user's behalf
Work identically over web and WhatsApp


Our stack:

React + TypeScript, Supabase (Postgres), and Claude as the AI engine. You should be comfortable here, but more important is that you understand how to build agentic systems well.


What we're looking for in you:

Proven experience shipping AI agent products (please show us)

Clean, reliable engineering — you care about it working correctly, not just shipping fast

Good communication and the ability to plan your own work and explain your decisions

Someone we can build an ongoing relationship with as Nudgly grows


To apply. Please include:

1 or 2 examples of your top agentic AI or LLM-powered products you've built, and your specific role

A short note on how you approach building reliable agent systems

Your availability and rate


A few important things:

First step is a review: Before anything else, you'll go through the work done so far and tell us honestly whether it's solid enough to build on or needs rework. We want your real assessment.

Strong English is essential. We communicate a lot, over meetings and in person ( if applicable ). so you need to be comfortable and clear in English.

We have a build plan to share. It's broken down into phases, each with estimated hours and an hourly rate. You're welcome to review it and suggest changes wherever you think it can be improved, we're open to your honest input."
